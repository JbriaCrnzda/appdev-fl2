"Starry Night's Spell: The Enigmatic Beauty of Vincent van Gogh's Masterpiece"

The Starry Night is an oil-on-canvas painting by the Dutch Post-Impressionist painter Vincent van Gogh, painted in June 1889. It depicts the view from the east-facing window of his asylum room at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an imaginary village.

"The Mona Lisa Mystery: Decoding the Enigmatic Smile of Leonardo da Vinci's Iconic Portrait"

The Mona Lisa is a half-length portrait painting by Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci. Considered an archetypal masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance, it has been described as "the best known, the most visited, the most written about, the most sung about, [and] the most parodied work of art in the world".

Marcos affirmed PH sovereignty over WPS in Shangri-La speech -analysts

During the Shangri-La Dialogue in Singapore, President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos Jr. delivered a strong statement affirming the Philippines' sovereignty over the West Philippine Sea, garnering praise from political analysts. Emphasizing the Philippines' status as an archipelagic state, Marcos highlighted the significance of the West Philippine Sea to the Filipino people, framing the nation as a maritime nation. He reiterated his administration's dedication to safeguarding the country's territorial rights amidst ongoing maritime disputes with China, signaling a firm stance on national sovereignty and maritime security..
